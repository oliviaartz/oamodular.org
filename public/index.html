Olivia Artz Modular is a place for art-objects created by trans artists. We’re currently focused on eurorack modules and other instruments.

Tele-touch is an expressive performance instrument designed for Monome’s popular Teletype platform. Gesture and nuance can be difficult to incorporate into modular performance. Tele-touch addresses this with beautiful copper touch-plates and high quality sliders. Buttons allow for mode-switching and triggering, making Tele-touch a natural place to control your system

Cycling 74’s Max and many I2C devices can also communicate with Tele-touch. [NEED MORE HERE]

Tele-touch 3 is available as a pair of PCBs for you to assemble or as a fully assembled and tested instrument.
